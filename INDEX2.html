Our Classes & More

Bharatanatanayam

Bharatanatyam – Tamil Nadu One of the oldest classical dance forms. Known for precise footwork, sculptural poses, and expressive storytelling.

kuchipudi

Kuchipudi – Andhra Pradesh A blend of dance and drama, originally performed by male Brahmin dancers. Features fluid movements, expressive storytelling, and dramatic characters.

Kathakali

Kathakali – Kerala A dance-drama form with elaborate makeup, colorful costumes, and facial expressions (Navarasas).

Mohiniyattam

Mohiniyattam – Kerala Performed by women, inspired by the myth of Mohini, the female form of Vishnu.

Manipur

Manipuri – Manipur Reflects the spiritual and graceful culture of Northeast India. Known for delicate movements, soft footwork, and religious themes, especially Radha-Krishna.

Odissi

Odissi – Odisha Highly lyrical and graceful, inspired by temple sculptures. Focuses on devotional themes, especially dedicated to Lord Jagannath.
